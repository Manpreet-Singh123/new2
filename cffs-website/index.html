Welcome to CFFS 2022!

Got a sciency idea for real world problems? Find it easier to code than to compose?
Maybe something along the lines of an app that can detect cancer, a liquid that can
turn everything to gold? You are in the right place!

This year, we, the Turing Club of IISER Mohali, in collaboration
with IEC(Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell) of IISER Mohali, invite
you to take part in an inter IISER hackathon. Only it won't be your usual hackathon.

Taking inspiration from iGEM (International Genetically Engineered Machine), we have
notched it up to increase the diversity of questions a hackathon can address.
In this hackathon, it will be you who gets to decide what kind of problems you want to solve via coding.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is this about, again?

CFFS is an open ended hackathon, where teams have to come up with their own science problem and then solve it. The only restriction being that coding must be a component of the solution. The pattern of the contest is loosely inspired from iGEM.

What kind of problem can it be?

The choice of the problem is up to you. The only restriction is that it should be a science problem from the domain of Physics, Chemistry, Biology or something inter-disciplinary, be it in the pure sciences or applied. And programming should be a component of your project.

Who is eligible to participate?

Since this is a pilot event, the organizers have restricted participation to only some sciences institutions in India. Any student enrolled in any of the IISERs, IISc, CEBS or NISER is eligible to form a team and participate.

What are the prizes for the winners?

There is a prize pool of INR 1.5 lakhs. Apart from that, for projects in applied sciences, there is a potential for mentorship support from iRISE, The Technology and Business Incubator if IISER Mohali. For theoretical projects, there is a potential of mentorship from one of our judges if they choose to pick a project for future mentoring.
